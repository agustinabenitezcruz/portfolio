The judgement

Role: Director, productor and screen writer.

A comedy short film based on the question: What happens after death? The entire production was designed as a 360-degree experience to be viewed through augmented reality headsets. This comedy short film was a major challenge to produce since it was our first time working in this format. For El Juicio, we chose a minimalist aesthetic to better guide the viewer’s gaze—overloading the entire space with information could cause the audience to miss subtle details that enhance the humor. Additionally, when writing the script, we considered that most viewers would be Argentine, ensuring that the humor and themes aligned with their cultural context.

Microorganisms

This is an experimental sound project where I used science as a basis to create the piece. Each color has a corresponding frequency, which I translated into a sound frequency. The vertical position corresponds to a musical note, while the horizontal position represents the spatial location of the sound. The theme of the piece is the population of a space.

Best experienced with headphones.
